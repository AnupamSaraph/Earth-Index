Join the Conversation

Join and shape the conversation to keep our Earth livable. There is much you can contribute to improve the interactions we have with each other, with other species, with ecosystems, and with the organizations that we create. Here are a few suggestions to build upon. Join the millions across the world who are striving to improve the our interactions, so life on our Earth may thrive and it may continue to improve its liveability.

Change the conversation to improve the liveability of our Earth

The conversations you create drive your interactions with other people, with other species, with ecosystems, and with the organizations we have created. Change those conversations to make our Earth more livable. For all life. Only when it is livable for all life, it will be livable for you. Here are simple suggestions on how you can change the conversations.

Celebrate and share the interactions people have with each other, with other species, with ecosystems, and with human designed organizations that contributed to improving the the liveability of our planet. Suggest and practice ideas for actions, policies, and culture that will improve the interactions people have with each other, with other species, with ecosystems, and with human designed organizations to improve our contributions to the liveability of our planet.

Use the Earth Index to start conversations on how your country can improve interactions its people have with each other, with other species, with ecosystems, and with human designed organizations. Learn more about the Earth Index and how different countries performed in the 2022 Report adapt the methodology to report the Earth Index of your city or organizations.
