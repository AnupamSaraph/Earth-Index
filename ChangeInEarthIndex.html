Change in Earth Index

Actions, policies and culture of a country alter the interactions its people have with each other, with other life forms, with ecosystems, and with their human designed systems and organizations. Decisions and actions are able to influence the short-term as they can change from year-to-year. Policies change more slowly and, therefore, can only influence over the medium-term. Cultures last long and, therefore provide resilience over the long-term. We report the change in Earth Index of countries over these time periods.
