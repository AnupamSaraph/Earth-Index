Earth Index

The Earth Index is an average of the scores for interactions people have with each other, with other life forms, with ecosystems, and with their human designed systems and organizations. It serves as an indicator of the investment a country makes to the liveability of our home, Earth. It provides a means to guide the actions, policies and culture that contribute to the changes in the liveability of our Earth.
